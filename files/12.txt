2. Cloud Management Platform: 
In these days, we can easily recognize that many companies are trying to automate their service, tasks not only to simplize the work but also to deliver a timely, accurate, and convenient service for their customers. It also applies to cloud computing management. A cloud management platform is a platform that can control and monitor the cloud resource. This is very helpful for businesses because it helps them have quick and general control over their platform.  
Depends on the vendor, providers that the application, service of the platform may be different, but generally speaking, this platform is mostly used for hybrid cloud models to centralize their control of the cloud infrastructure. An on-demand position for this field is Cloud Administrator who does the communication work with the cloud, control, customize resource, application for appropriate uses with the goals of the organization.
